Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

KPI Master helps teams visualize key performance indicators directly from Trello boards. This privacy policy explains what data is accessed and how it is processed when you use the Power-Up or our dashboard.

Data We Access

When you authorize KPI Master, we retrieve board-related data necessary to compute KPIs through the Trello API. This includes board names, lists, cards, members assigned to cards, activity dates, and comments.

How We Use the Data

We use this data solely to calculate and display metrics such as total cards, completion rate, active members, recently active cards, list funnels, and performance reports. We do not sell or share your data for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

The Power-Up uses Trello client authorization to read board data and render metrics directly in your session. Some configuration preferences, such as selected board ID, role preferences, and dashboard filters, may be stored locally in your browser's local storage to improve user experience.

Third-Party Integration

KPI Master allows you to optionally export reports to external workflow tools like Make.com or Zapier. This export only occurs if you explicitly configure a webhook URL and trigger the export action.

Revoking Access

You can disconnect KPI Master or revoke its access token at any time through the Power-Up settings in Trello or your Atlassian/Trello account connection settings. Once disconnected, the Power-Up will no longer have access to your data.
